RND - Remove Nameplate Debuffs - Clean Nameplates, Clear Focus!

The ultimate nameplate cleanup addon for ALL World of Warcraft versions!

[SIZE=4][COLOR=rgb(255, 145, 0)][B]What is RND?[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
RND removes all debuff icons from enemy nameplates, providing a cleaner, more focused combat interface. Perfect for PvP players, raiders, and anyone who wants reduced visual clutter during intense encounters.

[SIZE=4][COLOR=rgb(255, 145, 0)][B]Universal Compatibility[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
RND works seamlessly across EVERY World of Warcraft client:
• Retail (The War Within)
• Classic Era / Hardcore / Season of Discovery
• Cataclysm Classic
• Wrath of the Lich King Classic
• Burning Crusade Classic
• Mists of Pandaria Classic

[SIZE=4][COLOR=rgb(255, 145, 0)][B]Commands[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[CODE]/rnd[/CODE] or [CODE]/rnd help[/CODE] - Show all available commands
[CODE]/rnd on[/CODE] or [CODE]/rnd enable[/CODE] - Enable debuff removal
[CODE]/rnd off[/CODE] or [CODE]/rnd disable[/CODE] - Disable debuff removal
[CODE]/rnd status[/CODE] - Check current addon state
[CODE]/rnd test[/CODE] - Test addon functionality

[SIZE=4][COLOR=rgb(255, 145, 0)][B]Known Issues[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[COLOR=rgb(255, 0, 0)][B]Plater Compatibility:[/B][/COLOR]
• Issue: RND currently doesn't override Plater's debuff settings
• Workaround: Manually blacklist debuffs in Plater's settings
• Status: Full compatibility patch in development
