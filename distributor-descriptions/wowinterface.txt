The ultimate nameplate cleanup addon for ALL World of Warcraft versions!

[SIZE=4][COLOR=Orange][B]What is RND?[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
RND removes all debuff icons from enemy nameplates, providing a cleaner, more focused combat interface. Perfect for PvP players, raiders, and anyone who wants reduced visual clutter during intense encounters.

[SIZE=4][COLOR=Orange][B]Commands[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[CODE]/rnd[/CODE] or [CODE]/rnd help[/CODE] - Show all available commands
[CODE]/rnd on[/CODE] or [CODE]/rnd enable[/CODE] - Enable debuff removal
[CODE]/rnd off[/CODE] or [CODE]/rnd disable[/CODE] - Disable debuff removal
[CODE]/rnd status[/CODE] - Check current addon state
[CODE]/rnd test[/CODE] - Test addon functionality
